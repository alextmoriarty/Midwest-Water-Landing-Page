The Hard Water Problem

Does Any of This Sound Like Your Home?

Iowa and Illinois have some of the hardest water in the country. Most people just assume it's normal. It's not, and the damage adds up quietly.

🚿 Scale on faucet or showerhead images/symptom-scale.png
White Crust on Fixtures
Chalky buildup on faucets, showerheads, and around drains that keeps coming back no matter how often you clean it.
🍽️ Cloudy glasses or spotty dishes images/symptom-dishes.png
Cloudy Dishes and Glassware
Your dishwasher runs a full cycle and glasses still come out filmy. That's mineral deposit, not soap residue.
🧴 Dry or irritated skin images/symptom-skin.png
Dry Skin After Every Shower
Hard water strips moisture from skin and hair. That tight, itchy feeling after you shower? That's your water doing it.
👕 Rough or faded laundry images/symptom-laundry.jpg
Stiff, Rough Laundry
Towels scratchy right out of the wash. Colors fading faster than they should. Minerals in the water bind to fabric fibers.
🔧 Scale inside appliance or water heater images/symptom-appliance.png
Appliances Dying Early
Water heaters, dishwashers, and washing machines wear out years ahead of schedule because of mineral scale building up inside.
💸 High utility bill or excessive soap use images/symptom-bills.png
Higher Soap and Energy Bills
Hard water fights soap, so you use twice as much to get a lather. Scale in your water heater also drives up your energy bill every month.

Here's Exactly What We Test For

A real in-home test by a certified technician. Not a mail-in kit, not a guess over the phone.

💧
Water Hardness
We measure calcium and magnesium in grains per gallon. This is the main driver behind scale buildup and early appliance failure.
🔴
Iron Content
Iron causes brown staining in sinks, toilets, and laundry. We test both dissolved and particulate iron separately.
⚗️
Chlorine and Chloramines
City water is treated with chlorine. We measure how much reaches your taps and what it's doing to taste, smell, and your skin.
📊
pH Level
Acidic water slowly corrodes pipes and fixtures from the inside. We test your pH and explain what it means for your home long term.
🔬
Total Dissolved Solids
A broad measure of everything dissolved in your water including minerals, salts, and metals that you can't see or taste on their own.
📋
Full Written Report, Yours to Keep
You leave with your results in hand. No pressure to decide anything on the spot. We want you fully informed before anything else.
